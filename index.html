방탄소년단 Bangtan Sonyeondan BTS

What Is BTS?

BTS is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of 7 members; Kim Seokjin, Min Yoongi, Jung Hoseok, Kim Namjoon, Park Jimin, Kim Taehyung, and Jeon Jungook.

Breakthroughs BTS Has Done For Kpop

- First kpop act to preform on Ellen, Jimmy Kimmel Live, and the Late Late Show with James Corden

- First to have a Kpop album to peak top 10 on the Billboard Hot 200

- First kpop act to preform live at the American Music Awards

- First kpop group to win a Billboard Music Award (Top Social Artist 2017)
